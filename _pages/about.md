I study how media institutions adapt to digital infrastructures, with a focus on personalisation, recommendation, and the public value of news. I also co-lead the Diversity, News and AI lab with Prof Haiqing Yu.

Our cross-disciplinary team examines how AI shapes news distribution, user engagement, and democratic discourse. We focus especially on cultural and linguistically diverse populations.

## Current Projects

- **Civic Recommenders**: establishing new methods for normative scoring and civic signal modelling.  
- **Journalism and AI**: exploring deployment and use of journalism in the news media sector.  
- **5G and Telecommunications**: understanding deployment and adoption pathways.
